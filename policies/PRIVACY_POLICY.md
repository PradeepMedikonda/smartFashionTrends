# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 29, 2025**

## Introduction

Smart Fashion Trends ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered fashion recommendation system.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Username, email address, and encrypted password
- **Profile Information**: Fashion preferences, style choices, and sizing information

### Usage Data
- **Interaction Data**: Items viewed, liked, added to cart, wishlisted, and purchased
- **Ratings and Feedback**: Your ratings and feedback on recommended items
- **Browsing Patterns**: Categories and styles you browse most frequently

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, IP address
- **Usage Statistics**: Time spent on platform, features used, click patterns

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

1. **Personalized Recommendations**: Train AI models specific to your preferences
2. **Trend Analysis**: Identify fashion trends to improve recommendations
3. **Service Improvement**: Enhance our recommendation algorithms and user experience
4. **Account Management**: Maintain and secure your account
5. **Communication**: Send service-related notifications (with your consent)

## Data Training and AI Models

### User-Specific Training
- Each user's data is used to train personalized recommendation models
- Your interaction history influences the recommendations you receive
- Models continuously learn and adapt based on your feedback

### Privacy-Preserving Training
- User data is anonymized when used for aggregate trend analysis
- Personal identifiers are separated from behavioral data
- No cross-user data sharing without explicit aggregation and anonymization

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers without anonymization
- Use your data for purposes other than stated in this policy

We MAY share data in the following circumstances:
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect rights
- **Service Providers**: With trusted partners who assist in operations (under strict confidentiality)
- **Aggregate Analytics**: Anonymous, aggregated trend data for research purposes

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

1. **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
2. **Correction**: Update or correct your information
3. **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and associated data
4. **Opt-Out**: Disable certain data collection features
5. **Data Portability**: Export your data in a structured format
6. **Withdraw Consent**: Opt-out of personalized recommendations (with reduced functionality)

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption**: Data encrypted at rest and in transit (SSL/TLS)
- **Access Controls**: Strict access limitations to personal data
- **Password Security**: Passwords hashed using bcrypt
- **Regular Audits**: Security audits and vulnerability assessments
- **Secure Storage**: Database hosted in secure, compliant infrastructure

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Inactive Accounts**: Data archived after 2 years of inactivity
- **Deleted Accounts**: Personal data deleted within 30 days of deletion request
- **Aggregate Data**: Anonymous trend data may be retained indefinitely

## Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:
- Session management
- User authentication
- Preference storage
- Analytics (with ability to opt-out)

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## International Data Transfers

If you access our service from outside the primary hosting region, your data may be transferred to and processed in different jurisdictions. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes via:
- Email notification
- In-app notification
- Website announcement

Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests:

- **Email**: privacy@smartfashiontrends.com
- **Data Requests**: Submit through account settings
- **Complaints**: Contact your local data protection authority

## Compliance

This policy complies with:
- GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
- CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- Other applicable data protection laws
